RC24 Tri Series League Playoffs

Playoff Structure

How the Playoffs Work

The top 3 teams from the league stage qualify for the playoffs. The playoff system gives multiple chances to the top teams while maintaining competitive balance.

Step 1: Top 2 teams play in the Qualifier for direct entry to Final

Step 2: Qualifier loser gets a second chance in the Eliminator against 3rd place

Step 3: Qualifier winner and Eliminator winner meet in the Grand Final
